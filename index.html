Our Mission

Local

Preserving the elegance of a historic building means careful attention to local materials, traditions and landscape. Our architects unearth the wisdom of your area's specific styles, means and methods to keep your renovation in line with the enduring wisdom of long-standing practices.

Sustainable

Renewing your home to meet the best of modern standards, including Green Energy certified, low carbon footprint and recycled materials.

Beautiful

Our philosophy of mixing the old with the new to create something unique and contemporary while respecting the past ensures that your building will lose none of its character while gaining contemporary flair.
